Newsletters

During my time in college, I was a board member for a campus org called Circle K International. One of my main duties as the club's News Editor was to cocreate and design newsletters (the graphics, the backgrounds, the typography, etc.) throughout the year to keep our members informed/updated on everything going on within the club. A full look of our published newsletters can be found on issuu.
