Hi There!

I'm My Lynn (yes, my name has a space in it!). I'm a recent college graduate from UC Irvine with a degree in Computer Science. I initially came into college with almost no knowledge and only a slight interest in computer science, but throughout my time in college I've learned to see the beauty in it and hope to expand that appreciation into a career where I can combine my passions for creativity and tech into one!

Besides having an interest in technology & programming, I grew up really loving doodling and drawing. Once I was able to get my hands on a computer, my love for doodling translated into a love for graphic designing. Since then, graphic designing has become one of my favorite hobbies and I was able to make use out of this hobby throughout college by designing apparel, making flyers, and creating content for websites!

As for my general interests, I really love biking, watching YouTube videos, and watermelons! (I could probably eat a whole watermelon by myself in one sitting). Get to know me more by contacting me below!
